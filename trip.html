🌦 Weather & Roads

South Island spring weather and alpine roads can change quickly. Hooker Valley and Crown Range are weather-sensitive.

⚠️ Road Trip Notes

  • Drive on the left and allow more time than map estimates.
  • Refuel before remote or alpine stretches.
  • Keep warm layers, rain jackets, water and snacks in the car.
